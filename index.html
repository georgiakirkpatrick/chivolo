Kid Profile

When Chivolo's creator, Georgia Kirkpatrick, reviewed her four-year-old stepdaughter's history on the YouTube Kids app, she found that very few videos featured characters of color. Georgia created Chivolo with content from the YouTube Data API to provide families with kids' videos that reflect the diversity of the real world, helping children form positive associations with people of all races.

Profiles

Profiles help you curate content based on your child's age. At this point, the profiles you create will be erased when you close your web browser or refresh the Chivolo page. Georgia plans to update Chivolo to address this issue when she learns how to use servers.
